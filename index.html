English Private Tuition in Arucas

I offer English classes in Arucas (5 minutes from the school) most days between 10:00 and 15:00 at €15 per hour for both children and adults. Small groups of up to 3 students are also welcome at €10 per student.


If you want to boost your confidence in speaking or simply practice your skills, these lessons are for you.



Who am I?

I am an enthusiastic Polish tutor who lived in England for 17 years before moving to the sunny Canary Islands. I hold a Cambridge ESOL Level 2 Certificate in ESOL International (CAE – Cambridge Advanced English). I can also help with business English, as I have worked in various office departments.
